Rescue Skills Classes

Classes Offered

Swiftwater Rescue Level 4 - Cost $200

Supplying students with a strong foundation of the fundamentals allows them to build the pre-requisite knowledge for more advanced techniques. The ACA level 4 curriculum includes all of the essentials that every recreational to professional rescuer should be familar and comfortable with. By participating in this course, every participant will be exposed to the techniques and knowledge every rescue will employ

Suggested Requirements

Helmet, Rescue PFD and throw-bag
